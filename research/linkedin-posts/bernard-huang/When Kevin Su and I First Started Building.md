## Text Content

When Kevin Su and I first started building Clearscope 9 years ago (photo of us bumming some free desks from another startup), we set out to solve a simple but painful problem:

> How do you actually know what high-quality content looks like—before you hit publish?

Over the years, Clearscope became known as one of the best tools for content optimization. But the landscape has shifted.

Search isn’t just search anymore.

It’s AI chatbots. Generative summaries. Answer-first interfaces.

And a whole lot of uncertainty for content marketers and SEOs.

So we decided to build for what’s next. Today, we’re launching Clearscope 2.0 — a full discoverability platform for the AI-first era.

We’ve added:
• Topic Exploration to map your authority
• AI visibility tracking across AI chatbots like Gemini & GPT
• AI-assisted drafting crafted with your voice
• And a lower price point to make it all more accessible

This is the biggest update in Clearscope’s history. It’s also the clearest expression of what we’ve always tried to do:

> Helping brands get their content discovered, whether it's on Google, ChatGPT or whatever is next

To our customers, community, and everyone who’s been part of this journey—thank you!

More to come. Much more.
